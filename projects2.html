PayTogether Mobile App Design

PayTogether is a fintech concept app designed to simplify the awkward and often unfair process of splitting restaurant bills among groups of friends. Whether it’s vegetarians vs. meat-eaters or drinkers vs. non-drinkers, the app allows users to assign individual or shared items from the check with clarity and ease, ensuring everyone pays their fair share.

Project 3

Vecheck Client SMS view

I designed the SMS view for Vecheck, a digital vehicle inspection software that enables service centers to send detailed inspection reports directly to clients. This new SMS interface presents a clear, mobile-friendly summary of the vehicle’s inspection, including identified issues, recommended services, and tailored offers. Clients can quickly review all findings, select the services they want, and proceed through a streamlined checkout screen—all from within the SMS. The goal is to improve communication, increase service approvals, and deliver a seamless experience for the end customer.

UX Design Case Study TerraClean App

This project is a research case study UX Design. It is about the development of a volunteering app where people can find different events and join or sign up for them in their community. The research is based from interviewing volunteers and it is built by sketching and using wireframes method. The interview allowed me to get big takeaways on what people actually want to have featured on the app.

UI Design of TerraClean App

TerraClean is a volunteer app where people are able to attend various events. The name of the app comes from the word Terra which in Latin means earth. The app allows users to browse through different events and find something that they might like to attend. After the research, the app includes a list of events, their descriptions, date and time, images from the event, people who are interested in, reviews from other volunteers and an about us descriptions.
